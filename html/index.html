Kiriri Women’s University of Science and Technology is a private, women-only university located in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2002, its main objective is to bridge the gap in gender representation in higher education and contribute to the social and economic development of the country.

The university offers a range of programs, including B.Science degrees in mathematics, computer science, and business administration, as well as diplomas and certificates. With a focus on empowering women in sciences.

Kiriri Women’s University is the only women’s university in East and Southern Africa, providing a unique and conducive environment for learning and research.
